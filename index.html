Lunch Recipe for Students on a Budget ~ Chopped Chicken Caesar Salad Sandwich!

In today's post:

Find an easy lunch recipe for college students. This is a simple meal idea that doesn't take a lot of time or ingredients, but still tastes great!

I am a senior in college. Throughout the years I have tried many different lunches and I always come back to this one. The ingredients last throughout the week with no problem! It's filling, cheap, and stores well! You can pack it in a bag for lunch or eat it at home. Either way, it will leave you satisfied and craving more the next day!

I Introduce to You.... Chopped Caesar Salad Wraps

What You'll Need:

  • 1 heart of romaine
  • Precooked grilled chicken
  • Caesar dressing
  • 1/2 cupe of parmesan cheese
  • Croutons
  • Sourdough

How To Make:

Step 1: To make chopped caesar salad sandwiches, start by adding one chopped heart of romaine to a cutting board.

Step 2: Next, add one pound of cooked chicken on top of the lettuce. For this recipe, grilled chicken, but any cooked chicken can work!

Step 3: Add croutons ontop of the chicken and lettuce for the perfect crunch. Give everything a good chop!

Step 4: After chopping everything into pieces, place it all into a mixing bowl. Then, add shredded parmesean cheese and your preferred amount of dressing. Mix, mix, mix!

Step 5: Once everything is mixed, it's time to build the sandwiches! Grab your sourdough; although, any preferred bread will work! Place the mixture onto the bread.

Step 6: Lastly, add any preferred sides you may like. I usually like to add a side of fruit and some chips! And there you go!!! You're own caesar salad sandwhiches!

Once you try it, you'll be hooked!
