I Introduce to You.... Chopped Caesar Salad Wraps

What You'll Need

  • 1 heart of romaine
  • Precooked grilled chicken
  • Caesar dressing
  • 1/2 cupe of parmesan cheese
  • Croutons
  • Sourdough

How To Make

To make chopped caesar salad sandwiches, start by adding one chopped heart of romaine to a cutting board.

Next, add one pound of cooked chicken on top of the lettuce. For this recipe, grilled chicken, but any cooked chicken can work!

Add croutons ontop of the chicken and lettuce for the perfect crunch. Give everything a good chop!

After chopping everything into pieces, place it all into a mixing bowl. Then, add shredded parmesean cheese and your preferred amount of dressing. Mix, mix, mix!

Once everything is mixed, it's time to build the sandwiches! Grab your sourdough; although, any preferred bread will work! Place the mixture onto the bread.

Lastly, add any preferred sides you may like. I usually like to add a side of fruit and some chips! And there you go!!! You're own caesar salad sandwhiches!

Once you try it, you'll be hooked!
